HEATHCARE FACILITIES

Chitkara University in Himachal Pradesh offers healthcare facilities to cater to the needs of its students, faculty, and staff. Below is an overview of the healthcare services typically available on campus:

Medical Center:

The campus has a well-equipped medical center staffed with qualified medical professionals. It provides basic healthcare services, first aid, and initial treatment for minor illnesses and injuries.

On-Duty Doctor:

A doctor is usually available on campus during designated hours, offering consultations for general health issues.

Pharmacy:

A pharmacy or a medical dispensary may be located on or near the campus, where students can access essential medicines.

Hapiness Center:

Chitkara University is known for its holistic approach to education, which includes fostering mental and emotional well-being. The university has a Happiness Center, an initiative aimed at promoting a positive and balanced lifestyle among students, faculty, and staff.
