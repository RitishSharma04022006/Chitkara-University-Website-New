SPORTS FACILITIES

Chitkara University in Himachal Pradesh offers a range of sports facilities to promote physical fitness and recreational activities among students. These facilities cater to both outdoor and indoor sports enthusiasts and aim to encourage students to participate in a healthy, active lifestyle. Here's an overview of the sports facilities at the campus:

Outdoor Sports Facilities

Basketball Court

High-quality courts for recreational and competitive play. The court is designed to meet the needs of students, providing a space for practice, recreational play, and competitive matches. Chitkara University emphasizes the overall development of its students, and sports, including basketball, are an integral part of campus life.

Volleyball Court

Chitkara University, Himachal Pradesh, has a volleyball court as part of its comprehensive sports infrastructure. The court provides students with opportunities to engage in recreational play, practice sessions, and intercollegiate competitions. The university encourages active participation in sports as part of its commitment to fostering physical fitness and team spirit among students.

Cricket Ground

Well-maintained field for cricket matches and practice.

Indoor Sports Facilities

Table Tennis

Dedicated areas for table tennis matches.

Gymnasium

Modern gym facilities with equipment for cardio, strength training, and general fitness.

Badminton Courts

Indoor courts for badminton enthusiasts.

Carrom

Indoor spaces for board games and mind sports.
