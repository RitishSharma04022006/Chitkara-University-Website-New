Chitkara University is a well-known private university in India with multiple campuses, including one in Himachal Pradesh. Established in 2008 under the Chitkara Educational Trust, the university offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs, including engineering, management, health sciences, and media studies.
Himachal Pradesh Campus Location
The Himachal Pradesh campus of Chitkara University is located in the village of Kallujhanda, near Barotiwala in the Solan district. This area is in proximity to Baddi, which is an industrial hub, providing students with nearby industry exposure and internship opportunities. The location offers a scenic, peaceful setting with pleasant weather, typical of the Himachal region, making it an attractive place for students to study and experience the Himalayan landscape.
The campus is well-connected to nearby cities, especially Chandigarh, which is around a 45-minute drive away. This accessibility provides students with more urban conveniences while they study in a more serene, natural environment.

CHITKARA INFRASTRUCTURE

Chitkara University in Himachal Pradesh offers a modern and well-planned infrastructure designed to support academic excellence and student life. The campus features state-of-the-art facilities, including spacious lecture halls, well-equipped laboratories, and a library with a wide collection of resources. The university prioritizes technological integration, providing high-speed internet access and smart classrooms to enhance the learning experience.
The campus also boasts amenities such as residential hostels, recreational areas, and sports facilities, creating a comfortable and engaging environment for students. The green surroundings and well-maintained landscaping add to the campus's appeal, offering a pleasant and conducive setting for study and personal growth.
