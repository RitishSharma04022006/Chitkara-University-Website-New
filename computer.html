COMPUTER FACILITIES

Chitkara University provides robust computer facilities to support students' academic and research needs across disciplines. The campus hosts several well-equipped computer labs with high-performance computers and the latest software to accommodate various programs, from basic computing to specialized applications in fields like engineering, design, and data science.
Each computer lab has high-speed internet access, allowing students to conduct research, work on projects, and engage in online learning seamlessly. The labs are supported by dedicated IT staff who maintain the systems and assist students with technical issues. The university also emphasizes cybersecurity and data protection, implementing advanced security protocols to ensure safe and reliable access to digital resources.
