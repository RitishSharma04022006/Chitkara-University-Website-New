HOSTELS

Chitkara University provides comprehensive residential facilities that offer students a comfortable and supportive living environment. The campus includes separate hostels for male and female students, with options for shared or individual rooms, catering to a variety of needs and preferences. Rooms are equipped with essential furniture, such as beds, study tables, and storage units, ensuring students have everything they need for daily living and study. Solar water heaters, water coolers and water purifiers are provided in all the hostels. All rooms in the hostels have Internet connections. The pollution-free environment adds to the healthy living conditions on the campus.
